I'm a Lead Product Designer at Gogolook. I'm currently responsible for a small loan app called JUJI. Based in Taipei, I've worked with major clients in Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Southeast Asia — including Cathay United Bank, TSMC, Fitbit, and Genting Group.
